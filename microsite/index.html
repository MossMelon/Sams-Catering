Introduction

Welcome to Sam's Catering! Since 1980, Sam's Catering has been serving the greater Sacramento community for decades. We specialize in serving up delicious, mouth-watering, meat-and-potato dishes that make the perfect hearty meal for any event. Even better, our specially-selected wine specialists can recommend a tasteful pairing of wine or alcohol for your dishes.

Whether it be birthdays, bar-mitzvahs, weddings or corporate events, we've got you covered. Let us help you celebrate your event your way. Call us today!

Services

Standard Menu Bundle

We'll send you our famous tried-and-true classic menu for you to select your preferred dishes.This includes a total package of soups, salads, appetizers, sandwiches, and main dishes in a bundle, at our flat fee of $250.00 for up to 50 people. Groups of more will be an add-on cost.

Vegetarian Bundle

Though we specialize in meat and potatos, we understand the needs of our vegetarian friends. Let us serve you dishes from our specialized vegetarian menu, either in a bundle or as an add-on selection to your main meal.

Seasonal Special

Sam's Catering offers special discounts for parties of 200 or more people, on Christmas, Halloween, Thanksgiving, Hanukkah, and Memorial day. Ask about this special and we'll give you a 10% discount or more on your final price, depending on your party size.

Deluxe Catering Service

The Grand Royale of our catering business, Sam's Catering offers a deluxe catering service that includes not just our typical menu but our special gourmet menu. Our gourmet menu is prepared by our top-tier Italian and French chefs to deliver a fantastic dining experience.
